# 10 Prompts Executives Should Use

1. Start my day: “What changed since yesterday?”
2. “What needs my decision today?”
3. “Prep me for my next meeting.”
4. “Give me the patterns and risks on {topic}.”
5. “Summarize this long thread.”
6. “Draft a reply in my tone.”
7. “Give me three options for this message.”
8. “What did {person or team} say about {topic}?”
9. “Summarize this file into bullet points.”
10. “Rewrite this for executives.”
